-You get extra soft comfort and support because the thick seat cushions have a core of pocket springs and a top of cut foam and polyester fibers.
-The core of pocket springs is durable and keeps its form and soft comfort for a longer time.
-There’s plenty of room for everyone to sit comfortably thanks to the sofa’s spacious design.
-The cover is easy to keep clean as it is removable and can be machine washed.
-The viscose/linen cover is soft to the touch and has a subtle texture that creates a nice crinkle effect.

Designer thoughts

Designer Ola Wihlborg
"To really understand how comfortable NOCKEBY sofa is, you need to sit down in it or lie down. Maybe curl up in a corner or lean on the high armrests. That was my idea for the design, that I wanted it to be used in many different ways. I also wanted to make the cushions so soft that they embrace you, while keeping their shape and comfort over time. Because if you know how cozy and comfy this sofa is, you’ll want to spend as much time as you can there."
